Privacy

YAWA NOAA does not collect, sell, or share any personal data with the developer.

The app uses your device’s location to retrieve nearby weather conditions, forecasts, and alerts from public weather services including NOAA (weather.gov), Weather.com Personal Weather Stations, and WeatherAPI.com. Your location and API keys are used only to make these requests and are not stored or transmitted anywhere else.

All calculations and processing happen locally on your device. No accounts, analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.

Data Usage

YAWA NOAA does not collect or retain any user data on external servers. Weather data is fetched directly from the providers listed above based on your location.
